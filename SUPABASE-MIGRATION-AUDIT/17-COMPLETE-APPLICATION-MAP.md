### Viewpoint-Specific UI Customization Details

The application modifies KPI titles, values, speech bubbles, alert listings, and profile parameters depending on who is logged in:

#### A. Overview HUD KPI Card Swapping
*   **Rep View (`role === 'rep'`)**:
    *   *Card 1 (Relationship Health):* Renamed to **Active Customer Health**. Value computed as the average of the logged-in rep's customers (`custHealth%`, defaults to 94%).
    *   *Card 2 (Company Health):* Renamed to **Active Prospect Health**. Value computed as the average of the logged-in rep's prospects (`prospHealth%`, defaults to 85%).
    *   *Card 3 (Recovery Queue):* Remaining neglected accounts or recovery board records assigned to this rep.
    *   *Card 4 (Gifts Queued):* Renamed to **Logged Touch Points**. Value shows the rep's direct touchpoint count (`window.getEmployeeTouchpointsCount(username)`).
*   **Manager View (`role === 'manager'`)**:
    *   *Card 1 (Relationship Health):* Renamed to **Avg Customer Health**. Value computed as the average of all active customers under this manager's team reps.
    *   *Card 2 (Company Health):* Renamed to **Team Health**. Value computed as the average health index of the reps in this manager's team (defaults to 92%).
    *   *Card 3 (Recovery Queue):* Managed team accounts currently in the recovery queue.
    *   *Card 4 (Gifts Queued):* Renamed to **Avg Prospect Health**. Value shows the average prospect health score under the manager's team reps.
*   **Owner / Executive View (`role === 'owner'` / `'hr'`)**:
    *   *Card 1:* **Relationship Health** (organization-wide average customer health).
    *   *Card 2:* **Company Health** (organization-wide average health of prospects + customers).
    *   *Card 3:* **Recovery Queue** (total organization neglected/recovery accounts).
    *   *Card 4:* **Active Gifts Queued** (sum of `gifts` value across all organizational active accounts).

#### B. Cosmo AI Speeches & Insights Panel
*   **Reps / Managers (`role === 'rep'` / `'manager'`)**:
    *   *Cosmo Speech Bubble:* `"Marcus/Tom, here are your critical operational leakages and relationship decay warnings requiring immediate attention."`
    *   *Alert Cards:* Vanguard Health (Urgent - 34 days), Chevron Logistics (High - 42 days), Pinnacle Brands (Medium - 62% health, 68 days inactive) for Reps; Orion Biotech (Medium - 54% health, 64 days) is shown for Tom Collins.
    *   *Employee Audits:* Hidden (`#cosmo-employee-audits-section` display set to `none`).
*   **Owner / Executive View (`role === 'owner'` / `'hr'`)**:
    *   *Cosmo Speech Bubble:* `"Owner, here are the critical operational leakages and relationship decay warnings requiring immediate attention."`
    *   *Alert Cards:* Vanguard Health (Urgent - reassigning to CEO Paul K.), Chevron Logistics (High - executive outreach required), Dwight Schrute (Medium - 14 touches but 0% conversion rate).
    *   *Employee Audits:* Displayed showing team performance evaluations.

#### C. Alerts Hub & Task Cards (`#view-directory` Alerts)
*   **Rep View:** Shows **Representative Alerts**. Contains: Chevron Logistics Neglect Nudge, Tom Collins Performance Review, and Vanguard Health/Nova Financial Claim tasks. Button labels are set to `"Update Client Profile"` and `"Contact Owner (Gregory Sterling)"`.
*   **HR View:** Shows **CPO Escalated Operations**. Contains internal satisfaction audits, CEO alignment reviews, and wellness checkups.
*   **Owner View:** Shows **Escalated Operations**. Contains: Nudge Tom Collins (Chevron Logistics Red Alert), Tom Collins Performance Review Overdue, HR Satisfaction Sync (Sarah Lansky), and Claim & Reassign Neglected Accounts. Button labels set to `"Nudge Rep"` and `"Contact Rep"`.

## 7. COMPLETE DATA FLOW SUMMARY

### Data Source → Consumer Chain

```
users.json (auth)
  └→ login.html (validates email/password)
    └→ localStorage(whitebox_role, whitebox_username)
      └→ main-dashboard-v34.js (reads role, applies visibility)

employeesData (6 employees)
  ├→ Overview leaderboard
  ├→ Employees tab table
  ├→ Employee detail panel
  ├→ calendarEventsDB (gift schedules)
  ├→ Constellation map nodes
  ├→ Team profile detail
  └→ bonusRep4-10 (pads to 10 for owner view)

customersData (8 customers)
  ├→ Overview registry table
  ├→ Customers tab table
  ├→ Relationship detail panel
  ├→ clientGraphData (timelines)
  ├→ Recovery board (if inactiveDays >= decayCritical)
  ├→ Constellation map nodes
  └→ KPI calculations (health, neglected, total)

prospectsData (12 prospects)
  ├→ Overview registry table
  ├→ Prospects tab table
  ├→ Relationship detail panel
  ├→ clientGraphData (timelines)
  ├→ Constellation map nodes
  └→ KPI calculations (health, neglected, total, conversion)

analyticsDataSets (7 timeframes × 15 fields)
  ├→ Analytics tab KPI counters
  ├→ Health trend sparkline
  ├→ Gifting trend sparkline
  └→ Conversion funnel chart

leaderboardData (3 role types × 3 timeframes)
  └→ Overview leaderboard table

giftingDispatchSchedule (calendar events)
  ├→ Gifting calendar grid
  ├→ Gifting dispatch cards
  └→ Approval queue (owner)

calendarEventsDB (8 entities × send/receive)
  └→ Profile calendar view

activeAuditsDB (10 narratives)
  └→ Cosmo AI audit panel

liveActivityFeed (8 entries)
  └→ Overview live ticker

categorySpends (4R breakdown)
  └→ Spend donut chart

rmosSettings (15+ config values)
  ├→ Decay thresholds (warning/critical)
  ├→ Budget limits
  ├→ Approval gates
  ├→ Integration toggles
  └→ Webhook URLs
```
